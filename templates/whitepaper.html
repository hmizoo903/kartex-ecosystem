KARTEX Whitepaper v1.0

1. Executive Summary

KARTEX (KTX) is designed as a next-generation decentralized liquidity network engineered to resolve scalability bottlenecks, high transaction costs, and cross-border settlement frictions inherent in legacy payment infrastructures.

2. Core Protocol Architecture

By deploying low-latency state channels and dynamic liquidity allocation pools, KARTEX guarantees sub-second settlement times. The KTX token functions as the core utility asset powering fee mechanisms, governance staking, and cross-platform value transfer.

3. Token Utility & Economics

  • Liquidity Staking: Token holders provide liquidity to access algorithmic yield pools.
  • Micro-Transactions: Enables zero-friction, instant micro-settlements for digital dApps.
  • Ecosystem Governance: KTX grants voting weight on key protocol upgrades and dynamic parameters.
